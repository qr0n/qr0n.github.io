Intro

Hey, I'm Bhavesh Sharma — a developer with a deep love for building things that work and feel good to use. I’ve been programming personally for over 7 years and am currently a second-year university student. My projects range from AI-powered assistants to database systems and safety apps, often blending solid backend design with a bit of emotional intuition.

I’m the creator of Nugget, a modular, scalable voice AI bot architecture designed to run thousands of instances. I’ve also built systems like SafeMeet, an AI-powered personal safety companion, and IronDB, a custom key-value database loosely coupled with a high-speed cache layer.

I care about elegance in engineering—whether it’s through performance, modularity, or the way something feels to interact with. I’m currently exploring low-level systems (Rust/C++), database design, and AI-human interface design.

When I’m not coding, I’m probably sketching ideas for the next system, experimenting with LED setups, or breaking things just to understand how they work.
